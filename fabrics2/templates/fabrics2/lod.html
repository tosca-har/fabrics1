Measurement Definitions

These terms are used to define the relative abundances of mineral and lithic inclusions in the ceramic fabrics described in the Kuden database.

Label: Inclusion Dimension

IRI: https://kudenfabrics.anu.edu.au/vocab#inclusionDimension
Description: The range of proportions of an inclusion type in a ceramic fabric. The proportions are frequency counts, i.e. the number of inclusions of that type as a percentage of all inclusions, independent of inclusion size.
Subclass of: E54 Dimension

Label: Very Dominant

IRI: https://kudenfabrics.anu.edu.au/vocab#veryDominant
Description: The inclusion was 70-100% of the total inclusions.
Subclass of: Inclusion Dimension
Greater than: Major

Label: Major

IRI: https://kudenfabrics.anu.edu.au/vocab#major
Description: The inclusion was 50-70% of the total inclusions.
Subclass of: Inclusion Dimension
Greater than: Frequent

Label: Frequent

IRI: https://kudenfabrics.anu.edu.au/vocab#frequent

Subclass of: Inclusion Dimension

Label: Common

IRI: https://kudenfabrics.anu.edu.au/vocab#common

Subclass of: Inclusion Dimension
